3-2-1 Smoked Ribs

Ingredients

  • 1-2 Racks of Ribs
  • Meat Church BBQ Rub
  • Apple Juice
  • Brown Sugar
  • BBQ Sauce

Steps

  1. Remove silver skin and cover ribs with rub while breing brought up to room temp
  2. Set smoker to 225 and place ribs directly on grill
  3. After 3 hours remove from grill and place ribs in covered tray with 1 cup of apple juice
  4. After 2 hours remove from tray and cover with BBQ sauce, place direct on grill for 1 hour
  5. Enjoy!
