Macaroni and Cheese

Ingredients

  • box of KD
  • butter

Steps

  1. Add 2 cups of water to a pot and bring to a boil
  2. Once at roiling boil, add pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes
  3. At desired tenderess drain pasta and add cheese mix and 2 tbsp butter to pot, stir until incorporated
  4. Enjoy!
